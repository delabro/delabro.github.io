PROJECTS

These are a few of my most recent web projects.

Anti Police Brutality / NFPO

This project was aimed towards spreading awareness about police brutality. It was dedicated to Alton sterling (pictured) who was shot and killed after he was restrained by police officers mid 2016. The website features a number of shocking facts placed to expose to the reader just how grisly the situation is.

Police-Brutality Website

Spotify Brand Guidelines

This project breif was to create a 'brand-guidelines' website in which spotify was chosen to have their brand identity explained, as a sort of offering to anybody wishing to use their imagery to ensure consistency across the web.

Spotify Brand-Guidelines Website

Mudbrick Vineyard and Resturant

PROFILE

Some info about myself and my relationship with design.

My name is Aaron Delaveau. My passion for design spans a breadth of design platforms due to the nature of my interests and studies. Here's a brief background on these things.

I completed a bachelor of Design through Industrial Design at the Auckland University of Technology in 2014. From this I gained a global understanding of design and the importance of the creative process. - Along with a number of other design principles including a focus on UX design, as well as how to design within the guidelines of a given brief.

In 2016 I was exposed to the world of web design as I took on a Web Development course in which I learnt my coding skills across web languages and recieved my certificate.

My Graphic design skills were uncovered during secondary school and have been developed into what they are now through a general passion and interest for how things look.
